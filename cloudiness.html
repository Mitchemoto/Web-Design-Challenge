Latitude vs Cloudiness

The patterns of the data as it relates to cloudiness and latitude is an odd figure to review. The data strongly holds tight patterns at the same percentages all along the latitude range. This could reveal that there are many different storm fronts that pass through the latitude range giving varying levels of cloud cover. It would be interesting to review the cloudiness figures of longitude inconjuction with the latitude data to see if you cannot develop wind patterns and movements based on the two charts.
