Latitude vs Humidity

Taking a look at humidity as it relates to latitude, it is interesting to note that from some of the farthest reaches south, to the farthest reaches north, the humidity can still reach 100%. The clustering seems to be stronger in the northing hemisphere over all but the southern hemisphere has a solid cluster just south of the equater between 0 and -20 latitude. This could show a strong relationship holding a majority of data points between 80 and 100 percent and becoming more sparce as you branch out.
