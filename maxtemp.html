Latitude vs Max Temperature

As can be expected, the weather data shows that as temperatures reach 0 or closest to the equater, they tend to reach their peak temperatures. These seem to start to break within 20 degrees north or south. Oddly enough, the temperatures in the southern hemisphere seem to have less extremes that the northern hemisphere. The northern hemisphere does also seem to hold a greater diversity of points suggesting that more people inhabit the northern hemisphere, or hold more data collection sites.
